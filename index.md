## About Me

I am a final-year Ph.D. student in the Operations Research program at Georgia Tech ISyE. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xin Chen. I got an M.S. in Computational and Applied Mathematics from the University of Chicago, where I worked closely with Prof. Lek-Heng Lim. Before that, I obtained my B.S. in Information and Computing Science from Sun Yat-sen University.

## Research Interests

- Stochastic Programming, Nonconvex Optimization
- Operations Models, Optimal Control, Reinforcement Learning
